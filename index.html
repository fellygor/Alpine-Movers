Our Services



Home Moving

Moving into a new house should not be a problem anymore. We facilitate our Clients in relocating from one neighborhood to another or from estate to estate. We put extra care in handling of client belongings. The caring attitude is also professional and dedicated towards our customers' satisfaction.

Office Moving

Office moves are overwhelming. Alpine Movers removes this hassle by offering moving plans, establishing a schedule to moving based on our clients preference and one which will not affect the business day to day operations, unpacking and setting up your final destination.

Storage

Alpine Movers provides Storage and Warehousing services. We have devised customized storage solutions based on our clients needs. Our expansive storage facilities are equipped to handle any size of luggage for both residential and commercial storage needs.

Labelling & Packaging

Apart from facilitating our customers in moving, we offer packaging services. We provide packing boxes and wrapping papers for our clients. We enclose our clients products securely and with the direction of the client label them accordingly.

Courier services

Alpine Movers and Packers offers an opportunity for our clients to send parcels, valuable goods to many destinations within Kenya. You don't have to worry anymore about the safety of your goods as they ae being transported from one location to another.

Additional Services

Apart from providing basic moving services, our company offers additional services like wall mounting and DSTV installation at a really affordable prices.
